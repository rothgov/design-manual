Accessibility

The Equalities Act sets out the legal standards of accessibility that our services and information must comply with.

The council is committed to making the information and services we provide accessible to everyone in our community through all communications channels.

When publicity materials are being designed thought should go into ensuring there is a clear contrast between the font and the background colour.

Our products and services should be accessible to all. Accessiblity testing is currently taking place so this is a work in progress.
