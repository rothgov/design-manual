Introduction

Rotherham Council is a Metropolitan Borough Council and is responsible for providing a range of services, including:

  • social care
  • planning
  • housing
  • revenue and benefits support
  • licensing, business
  • regulation and enforcement
  • electoral registration
  • refuse and recycling
  • leisure and culture
  • parks and green spaces
  • economic growth
  • highways maintenance
  • education and skills
  • community safety
  • public health

It also has an important role in working with other providers of public services across Rotherham..
