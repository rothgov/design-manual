Logos

The logo should be placed in the bottom right hand side on all Council media if not used in the corporate footer at the bottom. It should ideally be in the same position as it sits in the footer.

A clear zone (white space) must be maintained around the logo.

The logo should always be used in its entirety and must not be altered, recreated or adjusted in any way.
