Our identity

Corporate identity is one of the key elements of good organisational communication. Applying the Council's identity consistently gives you the brand.

The Council's brand is made up of three elements: the logo, the colours and the font. How they are applied is important in creating a consistent look, feel and message. This should be applied across all of our communications.

This document is intended to provide staff, suppliers and partners with clear guidelines on the use of the Council's corporate identity.
